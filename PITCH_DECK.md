## Slide 2: The Problem
**Headline:** AI is built on unpaid labor.
**Bullet Points:**
- **The Issue:** Generative AI models are trained on millions of articles, books, and research papers, but the original authors receive **$0** in compensation.
- **The Gap:** There is no infrastructure to programmatically track when an AI model uses specific intellectual property to synthesize an answer.
- **The Result:** Creators are opting out of AI, hiding behind paywalls, and suing AI companies.
**Visual Idea:** A diagram showing AI models consuming creator data without any value flowing back to the creators.

## Slide 3: The Solution
**Headline:** Pay-Per-Prompt Citations.
**Bullet Points:**
- **CiteFlowAI** is a decentralized research terminal powered by an autonomous Agent Swarm.
- When you ask the AI a question, it retrieves registered intellectual property from our vector database.
- It explicitly cites the authors in its generated response.
- Most importantly, it executes a frictionless **Web3 nanopayment** directly to the cited creator's wallet—instantly.
**Visual Idea:** A clean flowchart: User Prompt ➡️ AI Synthesis & Citation ➡️ USDC Smart Contract ➡️ Creator Wallet.
